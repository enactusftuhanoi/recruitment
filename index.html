About Enactus FTU Hanoi

We are a community of student, academic and business leaders committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and shape a better, more sustainable world.

Our Mission

Enactus FTU Hanoi brings together a diverse network of university students, academic professionals and industry leaders around the shared mission of creating a better world through entrepreneurial action.

We believe investing in students who take entrepreneurial action for others creates a better world for us all. Our programs develop the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders and social innovators.

Since our founding in 2010, we've grown to become one of the most active Enactus teams in Vietnam, with over 50 active members working on 5+ social entrepreneurship projects annually.
